Key points in the world of Antidote
- Russia is currently in the middle of a civil war
- No one wants to try and interfere in the war due to potential backlash either during the war or after it finishes (seeing a superpower collapse on itself is kind of scary)
- The environment has continued to deteriorate
- Due to the changing environement the diseases that people have to deal with have changed too
- These are the ways the diseases have changed
	- Disease that were in previously colder regions have developed a better resistance to heat
	- Disease that werent in previously colder regions have moved there due to them warming up
	- New diseases have appeared with the changing environment
- Because of these the diseases humans experience have become harder to deal with and there is now a rush to develop medicine to help deal with these newly evolved/emerging diseases
